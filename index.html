Welcome to the Home Page of the
Experimental High Energy Physics Group at the University of Cyprus!

At the forefront of scientific discovery, our group is dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the universe through cutting-edge research in high-energy physics. Nestled in the heart of the Mediterranean, our team of passionate researchers, faculty, and students collaborates on groundbreaking experiments that explore the fundamental forces and particles that govern the cosmos.

With a rich history of scientific achievement and innovation, our group pushes the boundaries of human knowledge, striving to answer some of the most profound questions in physics. From the smallest subatomic particles to the largest structures in the universe, we delve into the unknown to expand our understanding of the fundamental building blocks of nature.

Explore our website to learn more about our research projects, discover the brilliant minds behind our discoveries, and stay updated on the latest developments in the world of high-energy physics. Join us on this exciting journey as we unlock the secrets of the universe, one experiment at a time.
